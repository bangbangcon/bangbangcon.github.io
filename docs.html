Hack and Tell. Show and tell for nerds.

Got a hack you want to show off? Here's how we do things.

  • You get 5 minutes to show off your hack.
  • Then you get 5 minutes to answer questions from the audience.
  • Your hack can use any language/platform/whatever, and can be almost anything that would belong in a vanilla hackathon. Have questions about what this means? Drop me a line.
  • It is free. Always will be. It would be antithetical to hacker culture to charge admission!

Hack and Tell Seattle is (informally) organized by this guy. Got questions? Feel free to email me at clemmer.alexander@gmail.com.
